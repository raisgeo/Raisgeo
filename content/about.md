I'm a geographer based in Kalimantan.

I currently work as Planning and Mapping Unit Head at Nusantara Climate Initiative, helping develop nature-based climate solutions across Borneo's forests and peatlands.

This blog isn't about my work. It's where I write whatever crosses my mind — books, activities, ideas, and the occasional observation about life, society, and the places around me.

I have lived in Java and Sumatra, but Kalimantan is where I think clearly.

Sometimes, writing about climate, forests, and the environment will find its way here too.

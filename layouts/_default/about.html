About

Hello, welcome to my personal blog. I am a professional who has consistently pursued geography for more than 10 years, from senior high school through my current career.

I currently serve as Planning and Mapping Unit Head at Nusantara Climate Initiative (NCI), working on spatial planning and mapping for nature-based solutions across forest and peatland landscapes in Kalimantan.

This blog isn't about my work. It's where I write whatever crosses my mind — books, activities, ideas, and the occasional observation about life, society, and the places around me. Sometimes, writing about forest, climate, and the environment will find its way here too.
